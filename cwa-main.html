Pretreatment

Section 307(b) of the Clean Water Act (33 U.S.C. 1317).

General prohibitions forbid discharge of any pollutant(s) to a POTW that can cause passthrough or interference. 40 C.F.R. § 403.5(a). Passthrough regulations are at 40 C.F.R. § 403.3(p). Interference regulations are at 40 C.F.R. § 403.3(k).

Specific prohibitions can be found at 40 C.F.R. § 403.5(b). See also 55 FR 30082.
